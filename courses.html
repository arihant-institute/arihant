B.C.A.(Bachelor in Computer Application)

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a three-year undergraduate degree program that focuses on computer applications and software development. It is a popular choice for students who want to enter the information technology (IT) field. The course is designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills required to succeed in computer science and information technology. The curriculum provides a sound knowledge of computer fundamentals, programming concepts, and applications. Students can get job opportunities in the IT sector after completing the BCA course. The course offer many programming launguage to learn and and use them to create web pages, mobile applications or even databases.
Course's duration is 3 years and is devided into 6 semesters.

D.C.A.(Diploma in Computer Application)

Diploma in Computer Application (DCA) is a one-year short-term program that provides an in-depth understanding of computer applications, equips students with practical and technical skills, and imparts knowledge on various computer tools and applications used in day-to-day life. The course is designed to primarily focus on the stream of students who wish to have Diploma in IT and good knowledge of Computer fundamentals and programming.The course involves the study of various computer applications such as MS Office, Internet Applications, Operating Systems, Database Management System (DBMS), HTML, and other related subjects.
The course's duration is 1 year and is devided in 2 semesters.

P.G.D.C.A.(Post Graduation Diploma in Computer Application)

PGDCA (Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Application) is a one-year academic course designed for individuals who want to pursue a career in the IT industry. The course covers a wide range of topics such as operating systems, database management systems, and web programming.The course provides numerous opportunities for students to opt for various PGDCA courses jobs such as web developer, web designer, computer language programmer, computer programmer, software developer, and ethical hacker.The curriculum covers topics such as critical thinking, technology application, empirical and quantitative analysis, communication, and teamwork.
The course is devided into 2 semesters lenthing 6 months each.

M.Sc (CS)(Master in Science Computer Science)

The Master in Science Computer Science program offers a broad vision of real-world business challenges and IT solutions to respond to them. The program provides students with a solid technical base, improves skills in software engineering, consulting, and management, and develops awareness of data science and cyber security issues. The program trains engineers to creatively and professionally apply information technology through socially and economically relevant applications, including apps for mobile devices, artificial intelligence, embedded systems, and scientific computing for supercomputers. The MSc in Computer Science and Engineering offers a high degree of flexibility in the students' individual study plan, and students can choose to specialize in one of the six predefined study lines. The program covers all the key areas of this in-demand subject, including innovation and project management, technology consulting, advanced programming, and machine learning.
The Course's duration is 2 years and is devided into 4 semesters.
